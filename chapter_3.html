Chapter 3

The Other Four Tickets

Charlie was the last child to find a golden ticket. Here's how the four other children found their golden tickets:

Augustus Gloop

The first child to find a golden ticket was Augustus Gloop.

He is a kid who loves eating candy.

Because he eats so much candy, he was able to find the first golden ticket quickly.

Veruca Salt

The second child to find a golden ticket was Veruca Salt.

She is a kid who loves toys. She loves toys and gifts from her father.

He enlists his entire factory to spend their time unwrapping chocolate bars until it was found.

Violet Beauregarde

The third child to find a golden ticket was Violet Beauregarde.

She is a kid who competes a lot trying to set a world record in chewing gum.

She takes a short break to find the ticket, then immediately returns to her world record attempt.

Mike Teavee

The fourth child to find a golden ticket is Mike Teavee.

He is a kid who watches a lot of television.

He finds the ticket but doesn’t even care to respond to reporters because he would rather watch his shows.
